Meal Planning App (In Progress)

Inspired by a nutrition plan provided by a personal trainer, this app helps users build meal plans based on balanced portions of protein, carbs, and vegetables. Users can create categorized food lists and select recipes, which the app then combines into meals based on portion goals (e.g., 6 oz protein, 8 oz carbs, plus vegetables). It features a backend server for full CRUD operations connected to a MariaDB database. Currently under development and being tested against real dietary routines.

About

Hi, I am Carlos, a full-stack developer dedicated to creating meaningful tools for people. After spending years on the forefront of AI—helping it learn to write and understand code—I've decided to make development my main focus. While I continue to keep a foot in the world of AI on my own time, my primary goal is to leverage my experience to build practical, user-centric applications that make a tangible difference for both individuals and businesses.

These days, I focus on creating clean, fast websites and custom software for small businesses. I handle everything myself - from development to hosting to ongoing support - with a focus on keeping things simple and efficient.

If you're looking for a practical solution without the bloat or overhead, I can help. Whether you're just starting out or rebuilding from something overcomplicated, I'll build something that fits exactly what you need - nothing more, nothing less.

I work independently by default and bring full projects to completion myself. For larger or more complex projects that benefit from specialized expertise or faster delivery, I assemble a trusted team of experienced developers and designers to ensure the best results on time and at scale. I personally oversee every project, managing the team and serving as your primary point of contact throughout the process.
