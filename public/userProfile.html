Hello! I'm Kai, a photographer based in New York City with a passion for creating impactful images that tell a story. My work focuses on the intersection of urban life and the natural world. I believe that every moment holds a unique narrative, and my goal is to capture those fleeting instances. When I'm not behind the lens, you can find me exploring the city's hidden gems or hiking in the mountains.
